Board game #1

Our first board game has a play-style like Risk and Catan combined. Players build soldiers to collect resources, expand their armies, and defeat the other players and to defend their capitals. There are three unit types: infantry, tanks, and planes. Collect resources, trade with other players, and be the last to survive in this epic new game!


Expected Release: Fall, 2020.

Unity 2D Top-Down Shooter

This game will have a play-style similar to Shovel-Knight, but it will be from a top-down prospective. The main character is a time traveler and he must defeat a single villain that is causing havoc across time and space. Time periods include: Renaissance, Wild West, WWI, Vietnam, Modern, and Future.
