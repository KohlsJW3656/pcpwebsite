About Us

What is Platinum Cherry Productions?

Platinum Cherry Productions is a gaming company built from the ground up. We came about from an idea of a terminal text-based game in high school. One man's idea turned into two very quickly. After the school blocked command prompt, the two owners hacked into the admin account to continue work on the game while at school. Eventually, the school found out and almost expelled them. Their dual-credit CVTC instructor talked down the administration, and the owners only had an hour of cleaning desks for their punishment. We now have a team of five hardworking individuals, ready for anything we throw at them.

What do we make?

We are currently working on a board game based off Caton and Risk. We have more board game ideas, and a very ambious idea of a 2D, top-down shooter game created in Unity.
